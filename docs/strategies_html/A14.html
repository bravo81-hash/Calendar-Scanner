Trader Intel (Pro Tips)

If the trade is profitable near exit, consider leaving a small piece (e.g., a 5-point wide butterfly) to expire. This creates a "free" shot at a max gain pin.

Event Risk FOMC & CPI

Avoid entering immediately before binary events. Volatility crush can hurt calendar hedges. Wait for the announcement to clear before entering.
